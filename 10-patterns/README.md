## 🎯 What to Expect
In this section, we will use our knowledge of **Loops (especially Nested Loops)** to print various structures like:
- Solid Rectangles
- Hollow Rectangles
- Half Pyramids
- Inverted Half Pyramids
- Number Pyramids 
- ... and more complex logical patterns!

## 💡 Why are Patterns Important?
Printing patterns is not just about making structural art in the console. It deeply strengthens your fundamental logical building blocks:
- **Inner Workings of Nested Loops:** Visualizing how the inner loop runs entirely for every single iteration of the outer loop.
- **Row & Column Logic:** Transforming grid-based coordinates into conditional outputs.
- **Problem-Solving Frameworks:** Preparing your brain for iterating over matrices, 2D Arrays, and Dynamic Programming in the future.
